FAQ

Is there a limit to how many accounts I can open?

No, there is no limit to the number of accounts you can open on our platform. Feel free to create multiple accounts to maximize your participation in various campaigns.

What is the minimum payout threshold?

There is no minimum payout threshold. You can request a payout at any time, regardless of the amount earned. We believe in providing flexibility and convenience to our partners.

How quickly will I receive my payments?

We strive to process payments promptly after verification, typically within 24-48 hours of your request. However, please note that processing times may vary depending on the payment method chosen and any potential verification requirements. Our priority is to ensure the security and accuracy of all transactions before disbursing payments to our partners.

Can I promote multiple campaigns simultaneously?

Yes, absolutely! You have the flexibility to promote multiple campaigns simultaneously. This allows you to diversify your efforts and maximize your earnings potential.

Is there a support team available to assist me?

Yes, we have a dedicated support team available to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have. Our team is committed to providing timely and helpful support to ensure your success.
